Welcome, students, teachers and staff of a large library! from the SSGMCE, Shegaon.

Do you have a textbook, a journal or a photograph that you need to receive the text from, but you don't have time to print it yourself?

Our Project will assist you in extracting text from a photograph. You may download the text and translate it using Google Translate, convert it to a PDF file, or save it in Word format after obtaining the result.

Text Recognition and Extraction It's never been so simple. Simply scan or photograph the text you want, choose the file, and send it to our text recognition service made with the help of Convolutional Neural Network. You will receive recognised and understandable text if the image with the text was clear enough.
